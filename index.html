A Day of Deontology - 11/17/2020

By Oliver Trejo

8:30 AM

Dilemma One

Do I join my meeting during the car ride to school? If I do, I will not be keeping my Mom company on the drive in, but if I don’t, I will be shirking my responsibilities.

I joined the meeting. Under my deontological rules, I should have joined it, since to not show up to the meeting would be breaking my word and would not be kind to the people expecting me to be there.

Dilemma Two

Do I do my English work in English class? I could either do other work or not work at all? Mr. Eaton did ask us to work on our projects, but I could not listen to him.

I did my English work. This was the most deontologiacally ethical action. Since Mr. Eaton asked us to do our English work, it would be considered dishonest and possibly selfish to not do it.
